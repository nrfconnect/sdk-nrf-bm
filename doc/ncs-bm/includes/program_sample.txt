Follow these steps to program this sample:

1. Plug in your device to the serial terminal or RTT using an USB cable.
   Your device appears on the Connected Devices View list in |VSC|.
#. To flash the application you have built, click :guilabel:`Flash` in the Actions View after connecting the device to the terminal.

For more details, see `How to flash an application`_ in |VSC| extension documentation.
