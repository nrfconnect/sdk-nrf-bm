To flash the S115 SoftDevice binary to your device, open the terminal and run the following commands from the :file:`nrf-bm` folder:

1. First, make sure that your device is erased:

   .. code-block:: console

      nrfutil device erase

#. Then, flash the SoftDevice binary:

   .. code-block:: console

      nrfutil device program --firmware subsys/softdevice/hex/s115/s115_9.0.0-2.prototype+offset-0x15D000_softdevice.hex
